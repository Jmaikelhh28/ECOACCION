Who are we?

At our NGO, we work to promote global citizenship by facilitating equitable access to renewable energy, especially through the installation of solar panels. We believe sustainable energy is a powerful tool to combat climate change, reduce energy inequalities, and empower vulnerable communities.

What do we do?

  • We develop solar panel installation projects in schools, health centers, and other essential infrastructure in under-resourced communities.
  • We collaborate with technicians and volunteers trained by the NGO, ensuring a participatory and sustainable approach in all our actions.

What do we contribute?

We provide access to renewable energy in vulnerable communities, reducing inequality and improving quality of life. We train local people and work with them to build a fairer and more sustainable future.
